Action gate (Veterinary Care Governor)

HARD holds cannot be overridden. A jurisdiction's licensing requirements, a case's own contraindications, its food-safety withdrawal-period sufficiency and its clinician's license currency are all independently recomputed, never trusted from the advisor's rationale.

OpGate
:case/intakephase-3 auto when clean
:jurisdiction/assessALWAYS human approval · requires an OFFICIAL spec-basis citation from veterinary.facts · a jurisdiction with no catalog entry is a permanent HARD block, no phase or approval can ever override it
:credential/screenALWAYS human approval when clean · a lapsed veterinarian license (self-reported or already on file) is an immediate, un-overridable HARD hold
:treatment/administerALWAYS human approval, at every phase · independently re-verifies contraindications, food-safety withdrawal-period sufficiency, evidence completeness, license currency and double-administration — any one of the five is a permanent HARD block
